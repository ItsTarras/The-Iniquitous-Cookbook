Shit I cooked : EGG SOUP - serves 4 food or something idk

INGREDIETNES\n

Shrimp - IDK like a couple dozen

cornstarch

Salt - 1 tsp (or 1/2 tsp of MSG)

White pepper

Chicken broth (homemade preferable)

Soy sauce - idk like a tablespoon or smth

garlic

spring onion (or scallions if you're WOKE)

a couple of eggs whisked (or beaten if they're into that kind of thing).

seasame oil

1. Start by defrosting your shrimp. You can do this by soaking it in a colainder placed within a bowl of cold water for around 10 minutes.

2. Create your base broth - if you're making your own, boil some chicken bones and vegetables of your choice - preferably ginger and onion.

3. Skim the broth of the fat from the top during this process every once in a while.

4. While the broth is being prepared, create a cornstarch slurry and whisk it up.

5. Whisk up a couple of eggs into a flask, which will allow it to be poured much easier later.

6. Pluck out the chunks from within the chicken broth - you want it to be a smooth silky soup with a hint of sticky thickness from the cornstarch.

7. Add the seasonings (soy sauce, salt/msg, pepper, garlic) and bring the soup slowly back up to a boil.

8. While stirring with chopsticks (it is important to use chopsticks or the dish will explode), slowly pour the whisked egg into the broth to achieve a ribboney texture.

9. Place the shrimp into the broth, making sure that they all hit their target. The odds are one in a krillion.

10. After around 1 minute, or when the shrimp turn pink, take the soup off the heat.

11. Add in around 1 tablespoon of seasame oil and the spring onion for garnish.

12. Serve the soup to your astonishingly large group of bitches (they all love your cooking).
